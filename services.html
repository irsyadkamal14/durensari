Services Offered at Duren Sari Sawahan Tourism Village

Duren Sari Sawahan Tourism Village offers a variety of tourism packages, including nature tours, traditional games, outbound activities, river tubing, culinary experiences, and homestays. Many visitors, including people from outside the city and tourists from America, Florida, and Canada, have enjoyed these packages. The packages range from one-day trips to two-day, two-night stays.

The nature tour package includes exploring the International Durio Forestry (IDF) durian forest, the largest durian forest in Southeast Asia, inaugurated by the Minister of Agriculture on May 13, 2016.

Visitors are taken on a journey through the durian forest, introduced to centuries-old durian trees, and can experience the refreshing Bawok River.

Another popular package is river tubing at Watulawang River. Visitors can enjoy river tubing with equipment that meets safety standards, including helmets, life vests, and arm and leg guards.

The river tubing adventure is guided by professional guides and monitored at every post. Participants will navigate the Watulawang River on tubes equipped with safety ropes.

Duren Sari Sawahan Tourism Village ensures a memorable experience by offering a blend of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural richness.

Visitors can immerse themselves in the local environment, enjoy traditional hospitality, and engage in various activities that highlight the uniqueness of the village.
