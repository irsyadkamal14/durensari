About IDF

Let's explore and visit the International Durio Forestry from Indonesia

IDF, or the International Durio Forestry, is the largest durian forest in Southeast Asia. The IDF durian forest is located in Sawahan Village, Watulimo District, Trenggalek Regency, East Java, Indonesia, covering an area of 650 hectares.

Exploring the IDF durian forest invites tourists to enjoy the durian forest guided by a tour guide. During the durian harvest season, tourists will be invited to harvest durians directly from the trees. Tourists can also enjoy durians amidst the forest atmosphere. Here are the achievements of Durensari Tourist Village

  • One of the best tourist villages in East Java in 2019 with the concept of CBT (Community Based Tourism).
  • Winner of the Hundred Tourist Villages Award (SADEWA) Trenggalek Regency 2022 for the Best Tourism Product Category."
  • Smart, Independent, and Prosperous Tourist Village at the Provincial Level in 2022.

Durensari History

Duren Sari Sawahan Tourism Village is a tourist village established on November 11, 2015, and inaugurated by the East Java Provincial Development Planning Agency (Bappeda). The Duren Sari Tourism Village has its secretariat at RT 12 RW 04, Singgahan Hamlet, Sawahan Village, Watulimo District, Trenggalek Regency.

Duren Sari stands out as a unique destination due to its vast and extensive durian forests, making it the largest durian-producing area in East Java.

CThe village is an oasis for durian enthusiasts, offering a rich and immersive experience surrounded by the lush greenery of durian trees.

Visitors to Duren Sari can expect to engage in a variety of activities that highlight the local culture and natural beauty.

Duren Sari offers a unique blend of cultural immersion, natural beauty, and culinary delight. The village provides a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, allowing visitors to relax and enjoy the tranquil environment.

In summary, Duren Sari Sawahan is a hidden gem in East Java, boasting the largest and most extensive durian forests in the region.
